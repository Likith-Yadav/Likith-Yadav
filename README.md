# About Me:
Hi! I’m Likith Yadav, a B.Tech AI & ML student at MVJCE, passionate about building intelligent systems and learning by doing.<br><br>
I’m currently exploring Agentic AI using tools like LangChain and n8n, while building projects around machine learning and predictions to deepen my foundation.<br><br>
As Technical Sub Lead at TENSOR, I help run workshops, mentor peers, and lead hackathon teams focused on AI and automation. I also have experience working as a Full Stack Intern at Innomatics, where I built and deployed AI-powered web apps.<br><br>
Beyond academics, I’ve completed freelance projects, delivering custom solutions for clients involving automation and web dashboards.<br><br>
I'm always looking to connect, collaborate, and grow especially in the AI and automation space!
