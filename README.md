# About Me:
Hi! I’m Likith Yadav, a B.Tech AI & ML student at MVJCE, Bangalore (2027), focused on building real-world AI systems and scalable applications.<br><br>

I specialize in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Large Language Models (LLMs), with hands-on experience in building intelligent systems, automation tools, and full-stack applications.<br><br>

Currently, I’m working on projects like a teaching-oriented LLM (BuildEx), AI workflow automation systems using n8n + Gemini, and ML-based solutions including autonomous navigation systems.<br><br>

As Technical Lead at TENSOR, I lead AI/ML initiatives, mentor 100+ students, and organize workshops and hackathons. I’ve also worked as a Full Stack Intern at Innomatics, where I built scalable MERN applications and optimized backend performance.<br><br>

Through BuildForU, I’ve delivered freelance projects involving automation systems, dashboards, and end-to-end deployments for real clients.<br><br>

I’m actively focused on becoming a high-impact AI Engineer by building scalable, production-level systems in AI, automation, and full-stack development.
